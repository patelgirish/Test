About

Hi. This Blog Portal is developed by Girish Patel, founder of Stern & Wise Management Consulting (S&W). The objective of this portal is to present the brief analytical summary of key policy and regulatory developments in the energy sector. The analysis would cater to the key judgments of Honorable Supreme Court, Appellate Tribunal of Electricity (APTEL), Central Electricity Regulatory Commission (CERC) as also the State Electricity Regulatory Commissions (SERCs).

About Stern & Wise Management Consulting (S&W)

“Stern & Wise” is founded in Delhi NCR to provide management consulting services in Energy and Infrastructure industries. The strategy of the firm is being planned to expand across all major industries and regions of India by providing – Strategy, Consulting, Digital & Technology Implementation solutions.
